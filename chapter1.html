What is Technical Analysis?

Understanding the foundation of market analysis

Technical analysis is a trading discipline that evaluates investments and identifies trading opportunities by analyzing statistical trends gathered from trading activity, such as price movement and volume. Unlike fundamental analysis, which focuses on a company's financial health and intrinsic value, technical analysis examines price charts, patterns, indicators, and market sentiment to predict future price movements.

Three Core Assumptions

1

Market Discounts Everything

All known information is already reflected in price

2

Price Moves in Trends

Once a trend is established, it tends to continue

3

History Tends to Repeat

Market patterns and cycles repeat over time

This approach enables technical analysts to use historical price data to predict future market behavior with statistical probability. By focusing on price action rather than fundamental factors, technical analysis provides a framework for timing market entries and exits based on observable patterns and trends.
